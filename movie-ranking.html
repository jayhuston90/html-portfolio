My top 3 movies of all-time.

He Got Game

The story of the pressures on the nation's best high school basketball player, could have been a gritty docudrama,but it's really more of a heartbreaker about a father and his son.

One of the most influential films of the 1990s, A delirious post-modern mix of neo-noir thriller,pitch-black humor and pop-culture touchstones.

Though well-acted and non-exploitative, a very well-worn story.
